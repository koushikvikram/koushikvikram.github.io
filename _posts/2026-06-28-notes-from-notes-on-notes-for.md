title: "Notes from vs Notes on vs Notes for"
categories: [general]
---

- "Notes from" - notes that originate in or are taken from a source. Eg. notes you made while reading a book/watching a video.
- "Notes on" - notes about a subject or work. Eg. reflections, commentary, or summary about something.
- "Notes for" - notes prepared for a purpose (a talk, exam, meeting, etc.).
