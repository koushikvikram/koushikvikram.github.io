## What

Techniques for bringing our lives back into balance.

## Why

When we're stressed, we're not only less happy and less productive, we make those around us unhappy as well.

## How

Short meditations on
- healing
- resting
- solitude
- being unbusy
  
These lead to the pleasure of relaxation no matter where you are.
