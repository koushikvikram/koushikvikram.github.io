Exploratory Data Analysis

In contrast to the purely theoretical nature of probability, statistics is an applied science concerned with analysis and modeling of data.

Inference is a sometimes complex set of procedures for drawing conclusions about large populations based on small samples.

Key drivers of the discipline of Exploratory Data Analysis are
  • rapid development of new technology
  • access to more and bigger data
  • greater use of quantitative analysis in a variety of disciplines
The key idea of EDA is that the first and most important step in any project based on data is to look at the data.

By summarizing and visualizing data, you can gain valuable intuition and understanding of the project.
